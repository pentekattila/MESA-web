Our concerts types

We are actively performing and making music and we accept every invitation we can. Essentially, we offer three types of concerts:

×

In this project, we are exclusively working on songs from hymnals. We started in the ragtime style, but now we have carried the idea further and are restyling the songs in styles inspired by 20th-century jazz. The goal is to transform the musical framework of the songs with humility but with such determination that it liberates the lyrics. By placing the lyrics in a new perspective, these songs evoke completely different feelings.

×

Worship is actually a guided worship service. We believe that the songs we choose will resonate as prayers. The essence of worship is singing together and harmonizing, so very often we become accompanists instead of leaders. It is uplifting to sing together, it is uplifting to pray together singing. We also fulfill these requests with love! We would also like to proclaim with this that we have many beautiful songs with which we can praise together in our own language, in our own spirit!

×

Although we have heard so many times what the Christmas holiday is all about, that it is almost unnecessary to say every word. Still, in our Christmas concert, we tried to create an occasion where Christmas songs or songs suitable for the occasion are performed in a clearly MÉSA atmosphere and style, but the goal is to achieve silence and facilitate the birth. So that the Savior may be reborn in everyone, for this is the true message of Christmas! We are eagerly awaiting the intimate magic of preparing together and celebrating together.

Each concert consists of minimum 30 minutes, however we are also open to special requests. We warmly welcome every invitation and strive to accept each request. Those who have already heard us and felt their hearts beat along with us are encouraged to share their experiences- and for those who have not yet met us, we eagerly await the opportunity to connect.
