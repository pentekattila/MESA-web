Who are we?

What is MÉSA? Most of you might know us as Merci and Atti. For a long time, we felt the need to give form to our duo, and this is how MÉSA was born. We are returning after a two-year break because the Lord has placed this on our hearts. MÉSA is a search; MÉSA is a question. We seek God in music, form, text, and fusion. We seek the divine that shakes us, the clearly but not rigidly defined, the Christ-like innovation, and openness. We are constantly searching, and sometimes we find; what we find, we share with you, with love.
