Why?

We feel it is important to speak honestly and without hypocrisy about everything we can. Therefore, we also consider it essential to place what we do in the right context. What we do is not the music of servants, but the service of musicians. As musicians, we have chosen to praise God. We want to do this in a way that is attractive and attention-grabbing even for non-believers. This can only be achieved by working at the highest possible musical level. We truly want to bring quality and something new into this musical dimension. We humbly but firmly believe this; however, to truly accomplish all of this with the highest quality, we also need support. If you love what we do and would generously contribute to this, here is how you can support us.

There is no doubt that we would like to seal our Ragtime project with an album. However, an album would not merely mean a tangible CD. The album actually encompasses everything that comes with it: a studio-quality recording of these songs, but it also means that doors will open to online streaming platforms. If you would like to hold this album in your hands or listen to us on Spotify or other platforms, you can support us in making this happen.

It was never a question for us that MÉSA should be socially uplifting. That's why we want to bring good news, happiness, and music even to places where there are rarely such opportunities. We want to reach out to financially destitute or socially disadvantaged congregations and communities where extraordinary events cannot otherwise be created. Of course, this also entails costs, but our goal is to cover these costs from the donations of our wealthier supporters. If it's important to you that we reach out to those living in isolation, hardship or loneliness, then you can support us in this.
